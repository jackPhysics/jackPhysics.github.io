Before you visit these pages note there are various key-board shortcuts available to help (press the HELP button to find out more).
There are buttons available lower down for calling up CRTs, TECs etc or a help sheet that lists the keyboard short cuts. Most of the shortcuts can also be accessed through the 'hopper' which appears whenever you click on a piece.
Pieces can be moved by dragging or by clicking to choose and then clicking on where you want them to go.

The most important shortcut to know at the start is the "1" key shrinks the board and pieces (zoom out),
and the "2" expands the board and pieces (zoom in).

Play the original game or try out new armies and/or new boards.
It's best to select new armies/boards before starting to play - changing halfway through a game could lead to unpredictable results!
