Play Here!

Before you visit these pages note there are various key-board shortcuts available to help (press the HELP button to find out more).
There are buttons available lower down for calling up CRTs, TECs etc or a help sheet that lists the keyboard short cuts. Most of the shortcuts can also be accessed through the 'hopper' which appears whenever you click on a piece.
Pieces can be moved by dragging or by clicking to choose and then clicking on where you want them to go.

The most important shortcut to know at the start is the "1" key shrinks the board and pieces (zoom out),
and the "2" expands the board and pieces (zoom in).

SPI'S PRESTAGS (1976)

Playtesting Prestags

This module allows you to choose from all the Prestags boards but also many other boards (eg Dark Ages, Kampfpanzer! etc.)
- you can turn terrain features on or off and use part of the board or all of the board
(use the x++/x-- and y++/y-- buttons to move the board around)
(use the width+- and height+- buttons to use more or less of the boards)

It is designed as a 'playtest' space and so armies are all one type or a limited range of types (every force is 24 points)
so rules governing ranged fire, cavalry movement, infantry classes, weapon effectiveness, chariots, slopes etc can all be judged in isolation.

Try SD v BI, or HC v LB, or MI v PP...

There are no leader units in this module.

On opening, the 1st forces presented are not a real force but an example of every possible piece in the original game.

Play Prestags

This is the 'Playtesting' module (see above) adapted to play actual scenarios (4 at the moment but more will become available).

Press the "Setup Game" button (bottom left) and it will cycle through the scenarios available.

This module has leader units (they can be changed with the 'C' button, and some units can be changed (eg LB to AX), MC and HC can dismount.

You can also choose the individual armies and choose your own board to create your own scenarios.

Play Prestags v2

As above but here there is a single button that chooses the scenario directly.

You will need 'pop-ups enabled' as the scenario instructions appear within a pop-up.

There is a button ('situation') to re-open the situation window if you close it.

Individual PRESTAGS scenarios:

Pressing the 'Setup Game' button near the bottom left will move pieces into starting positions - pressing more than once may give alternative starting positions.

The 'playtesting' module renders board and pieces on the fly using javascript, this module uses .jpg, .png or .gif images of the boards and pieces.

These versions allow the changing of units (eg LB to AX, dismounting cavalry etc) - use the 'C' button
