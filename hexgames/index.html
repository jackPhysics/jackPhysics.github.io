Play Here!

Before you visit these pages note there are various key-board shortcuts available to help.
There are also buttons available lower down for calling up CRTs, TECs etc or a help sheet that lists the keyboard short cuts. Most of the shortcuts can also be accessed through the 'hopper' which appears whenever you click on a piece.
Pieces can be moved by dragging or by clicking to choose and then clicking on where you want them to go.

The most important shortcut to know at the start is the "1" key shrinks the board and pieces (zoom out),
and the "2" expands the board and pieces (zoom in).

SPI'S PRESTAGS (1976)

Playtesting Prestags

This module allows you to choose the 'Chariot', 'Spartan' or 'Yeoman' board
- you can turn terrain features on or off and use part of the board or all
(use the x+-5 and y+-5 buttons to move the board around)
(use the width+- and height+- buttons to use more or less of the boards)

It is designed as a 'playtest' space and so armies are all one type or a limited range of types (every army is 24 points)
so rules governing ranged fire, cavalry movement, infantry classes etc can all be judged in isolation.

I'll soon develop a similar module that includes actual armies from tthe differentt scenarios so that different scenarios can be played.

Prestags: Hattin

Pressing the 'Setup Game' butto near the bottom leftt willl mov pieces into starting positions - it can often be pressed more than once to give alternative starting positions.
